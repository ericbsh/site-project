About us

Brand story

2021 the big community company announced the concept of the metaverse. The founder Eric Almighty, who has been passionate about architecture since he was a child, convinced his friend Ben Johnson, who is engaged in the Internet software industry, to founded MWA Inc together on 2022/01/01. The company name - Milky Way Architecture was named because Eric has always had the dream of building a house on other planets. Nowadays, in the advanced world, Eric can not only fulfill his dream but also become the promoter of this industry!

Vision

We are the first company in Taiwan to sell buildings in the form of NFT. Our vision is to become the world's number one virtual real estate company after Metaverse matures and stabilizes!

Mission

Our mission is to design and manufacture virtual homes that satisfy our customers. NTF cannot be changed after it is published on the platform, so we provide customized services before issuing it. The two parties reach a consensus in the form of a contract and then release the product to the platform after signing the contract.

Value

Make the thing possible without the thinking limited and get into the new world with fearless and passion. Our slogan: "Build your home in the Metaverse and make your second greater life!"

How people live in the Metaverse

Metaverse is a whole new world for people, we can do anything without the limit here. In the future, just one device on our head or wear glasses and we can get into a virtual environment. Therefore, remoting work, having a meeting, studying, and even hanging out with friends. All these things we can accomplish in the Metaverse. Let's see the film for realizing more.
