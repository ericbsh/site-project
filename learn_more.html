Metaverse

Metaverse is based on virtual imaging technologies such as VR, AR, MR, XR, etc., to create a realistic world. People can complete the needs of clothing, housing, transportation, education, entertainment, etc., in the virtual world. So far, various technology giants are building their own metaverse, and in the end, the largest one will be the mainstream, and more and more people will join it.

NFT

NFT (Non-fungible-token) is a smart contract concept, which can be understood as literally, the fungible-token is Ether. On the other hand, virtual assets exist in the form of NFT, such as virtual real estate, virtual clothing, virtual artwork, and so on. And different virtual assets have different standard specifications. E.g. ERC-721.

Ethereum

Ethereum is an extension technology based on blockchain. Ethereum is a public blockchain platform created by Vitalik Buterin. It includes Ethereum blockchain technology (a first-generation blockchain) and a smart contract area. Blockchain technology (second-generation blockchain). This technology mainly lets information decentralized, makes data public and transparent and is difficult to be tampered with.
